Paris

About the Destination

Paris is a world-renowned capital of art, fashion, and culture, often referred to as the "City of Light." Situated along the banks of the Seine River, it offers a timeless experience, from the romantic streets of Montmartre to the grand boulevards designed by Haussmann.

The city is globally famous for its iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum. Beyond the monuments, visitors can enjoy world-class pastry at local patisseries or take a scenic stroll through the Luxembourg Gardens.

Historical Significance

With its origins as a Celtic settlement, Paris has grown into one of the world's leading business and cultural centers. The city's history is deeply woven into the fabric of Western civilization, seen in its revolutionary spirit and its preservation of historic districts like Le Marais.

Fast Facts

  • Language: French
  • Currency: Euro (€)
  • Transport: Metro, RER, Velib
